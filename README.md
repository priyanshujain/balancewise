# BalanceWise

A simple app to help you live a more balanced life.

## What is BalanceWise?

BalanceWise is designed for anyone looking for assistance in maintaining a healthy, balanced lifestyle. Whether you're trying to stay on top of your fitness goals, eat better, or build consistent habits, this app aims to help you track and achieve your wellness objectives.

## Features

### 1. Tasks & Goals
Set daily, weekly, and monthly goals to build healthy habits. Get timely reminders to help you stay on track with your wellness journey.

### 2. Diet Journaling with AI
Log your meals effortlessly with AI-powered food recognition. Simply take a photo of your food, and the app will automatically parse the details for you, making nutrition tracking simple and intuitive.

### 3. Exercise Tracking
Create your personalized workout schedule and record your exercise sessions with built-in timers. Track your fitness progress and maintain consistency in your workout routine.
